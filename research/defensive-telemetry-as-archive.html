RESEARCH_001

Defensive TelemetryAs Archive

STATUS://DRAFT SIGNAL://FORENSIC FORMAT://ESSAY

This page is a publish-ready placeholder for a longer study on logs, packet traces, alerts, and sensor residue as archival material.

Abstract

Security telemetry is often treated as an operational stream. This study reframes it as a memory system: partial, noisy, time-bound, and shaped by the instruments that captured it.

Working Questions

What does a security system remember when its records are incomplete? Which traces become evidence, and which disappear into retention windows, alert thresholds, and collection blind spots?
