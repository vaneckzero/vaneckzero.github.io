VANECKZERO site navigation notes

Transmissions
Featured art and media works: glitch video, CRT/photo studies, projection pieces, and major portfolio objects.

Archive
A browsable catalog/index of individual artifacts: videos, photos, research notes, drafts, studies, and recovered items. This is the expandable database-like part of the site.

Live Systems
Performance and installation systems: generative visuals, CRT routing, projection mapping, telemetry overlays, and live capture chains.

Signal Studies
Experimental sketches and conceptual bridges between cybersecurity, signal processing, media decay, telemetry, and image systems.

Research
Longer written cybersecurity/media essays. This is where publishable articles, methodology notes, case studies, and theory pieces live.

About
Artist/project identity, practice description, contact links, and eventually socials, booking, and collaboration information.
